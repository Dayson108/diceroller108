Incendiary Cloud(C)

Conjuration Cantrip
Castable by: Sorcerer Wizard
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 150 feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Minute
Source: Page: 253 Players Handbook

A swirling cloud of smoke shot through with white-hot embers appears in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within range.

The cloud spreads around corners and is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it.

When the cloud appears, each creature in it must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 10d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The cloud moves 10 Feet directly away from you in a direction that you choose at the start of each of your turns.
