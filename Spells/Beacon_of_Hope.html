Beacon of Hope(C)

Abjuration Cantrip
Castable by: Cleric
Components: V, S
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Minute
Source: Page: 217 Players Handbook

This spell bestows hope and vitality. Choose any number of creatures within range. For the duration, each target has advantage on Wisdom saing throws and death saving throws, and regians the maximum number of hit points possible from any healing.
