Wall of Ice(C)(+)

Evocation Cantrip
Castable by: Wizard
Components: V, S, M (a small piece of quartz)
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 120 feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 Minutes
Source: Page: 285 Players Handbook
