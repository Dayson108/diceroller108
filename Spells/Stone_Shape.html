Stone Shape

Transmutation Cantrip
Castable by: Cleric Druid Wizard
Components: V, S, M soft clay, which must be worked into roughly the desired shape of the stone object)
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Instantaneous
Source: Page: 278 Players Handbook

You touch a stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 5 feet in any dimension and form it into any shape that suits your purpose. So, for example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, idol, or coffer, or make a small passage through a wall, as long as the wall is less than 5 feet thick.
